What is Power BI

Power BI is a collection of software services, apps, and connectors that work together to turn your unrelated sources of data into coherent, visually immersive, and interactive insights. Your data might be an Excel spreadsheet, or a collection of cloud-based and on-premises hybrid data warehouses. Power BI lets you easily connect to your data sources, visualize and discover what’s important, and share that with anyone or everyone you want.

Why Power BI?

Power BI is a common business intelligence tool used by business analysts and professionals. It is designed to be easily accessible for those without any specialized data knowledge. It is used for creating reports and dashboards that present data sets in multiple ways using visuals, connecting various data sources, such as Excel sheets, onsite data warehouses, and cloud-based data storage, and then transforming them into business insights.

ADVANTAGES OF POWER BI

Affordability: Power BI is relatively inexpensive compared to other BI tools in the market. It also offers a free version with limited capabilities. Custom Visualizations: Power BI allows users to create custom visualizations and reports according to their needs. Excel Integration: Power BI integrates seamlessly with Excel, allowing users to import data and create reports using familiar tools.

DISADVANTAGES OF POWER BI

Cost: While Power BI is affordable, the cost can add up for larger organizations as each user requires a license. Steep Learning Curve: Power BI can be complex and difficult to learn for beginners. Limited Customization: Although Power BI allows for custom visualizations, it has limitations and may not be as flexible as some other BI tools.

Our facilities

Power BI provides a wide range of facilities to help you turn your data into actionable insights. Here are some of the key facilities offered by Power BI:

Connectivity to a variety of data sources

Power BI can connect to a wide range of data sources, from Excel spreadsheets and on-premises SQL Server databases to cloud services like Azure SQL Database and Salesforce.

Data modeling and transformation

Power BI provides tools for transforming raw data into a format that's suitable for reporting and analysis. You can clean, transform, and reshape your data before loading it into Power BI.

Customizable visualizations and dashboards

Power BI allows you to create interactive reports and dashboards with a variety of visualizations. You can customize the look and feel of these visualizations to suit your needs.
